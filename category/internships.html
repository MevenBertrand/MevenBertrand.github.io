  1. Gradualizing the Calculus of Inductive Constructions

    January 2019 – July 2019
    Gallinette team (University of Nantes), with Nicolas Tabareau

    I spent the spring semester 2019 as an intern under the supervision of Nicolas Tabareau. We worked with Éric Tanter on applying the gradual typing approach to the Calculus of Inductive Constructions. Although the internship led to a thesis and defense, those are outdated – see the POPL '21 paper instead.

  3. Compilation of Dependent Pattern-Matching without Axiom K

    June 2016 – July 2016
    IRIF (Paris 7 University), with Hugo Herbelin

    In 2016, I was an intern for 2 months with Hugo Herbelin. We worked on an algorithm to compile complex, dependent pattern-matching without using the axiom K, following ideas around small inversion. It did not make it to any kind of publication for now.
